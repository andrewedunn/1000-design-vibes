Our Story

How we fell in love

2019

First Meeting

We met at a mutual friend's architecture gallery opening in Oakland. Emma was admiring a mid-century modern chair, and Michael made a joke about its impracticality. We talked for three hours about design, travel, and our shared love of terrible puns.

2020

First Adventure

Our first trip together was to Portland. We spent three days exploring vintage furniture shops, eating our way through the city, and getting caught in the rain without umbrellas. Michael says this is when he knew.

2022

Moving In Together

After two years of splitting time between San Francisco and Berkeley, we found the perfect apartment in Oakland with big windows and hardwood floors. We spent months furnishing it with pieces from estate sales and arguing lovingly about paint colors.

2024

The Proposal

Michael proposed on a quiet Sunday morning in our living room, surrounded by the furniture we'd collected together. No grand gestures, just us, coffee, and a simple question. Emma said yes immediately (and then cried for 20 minutes).

2026

Getting Married

And now we're here, ready to celebrate with the people who matter most. Thank you for being part of our journey. We can't wait to dance with you in Sonoma.
