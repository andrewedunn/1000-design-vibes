About This Workshop

Join us for a transformative day-long workshop where educators learn to integrate design thinking methodologies into their teaching practice. This hands-on experience will equip you with practical tools to foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking in your students. Through interactive sessions, real-world case studies, and peer collaboration, you'll discover how to create student-centered learning experiences that inspire innovation and prepare learners for the challenges of tomorrow.

What You'll Learn

  • Human-centered design principles
  • Rapid prototyping techniques
  • Student empathy mapping
  • Collaborative ideation methods
  • Classroom implementation strategies
