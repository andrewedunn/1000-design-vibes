Things to Do

Explore Lisbon's neighborhoods: Alfama, Bairro Alto, Belém.

Must-see: São Jorge Castle, Jerónimos Monastery, LX Factory, Miradouro da Senhora do Monte.

Pro tip: Get a 24-hour tram pass and ride the iconic Tram 28.

Weather & Packing

June in Lisbon: 70-80°F (21-27°C), sunny with occasional evening breeze.

Pack light layers, comfortable walking shoes, sunscreen, and sunglasses.

The ceremony is outdoors but covered. Reception is open-air rooftop.

Visa & Passport

US and EU citizens: No visa required for stays under 90 days.

Passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond travel dates.

Check requirements for your country at the Portuguese embassy website.
