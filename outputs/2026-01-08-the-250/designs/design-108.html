Advanced Cardiac Life Support: Critical Decision Making

Dr. Elena Vasquez 3,847 views Updated Dec 2025 CME Certified

This comprehensive training module covers evidence-based protocols for managing cardiac emergencies in critical care settings. Through detailed case studies and simulation scenarios, healthcare professionals will enhance their ability to make rapid, accurate decisions during high-pressure resuscitation events. Topics include rhythm recognition, pharmacological interventions, post-cardiac arrest care, and team coordination strategies.
