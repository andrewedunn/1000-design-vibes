Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response to my ticket?
+
Our support team typically responds within 2-4 hours during business hours (9am-6pm EST, Monday-Friday). Urgent tickets are prioritized and usually receive attention within 1 hour. For after-hours support, responses may take up to 24 hours.
Can I track the status of my ticket?
+
Yes! Once you submit a ticket, you'll receive a confirmation email with a unique ticket ID. You can log into your account and navigate to "My Tickets" to view real-time status updates and any communications from our support team.
What information should I include in my ticket?
+
Please provide a clear description of your issue, including any error messages you've encountered, steps to reproduce the problem, and screenshots if applicable. The more detail you provide, the faster we can resolve your issue.
Do you offer phone or live chat support?
+
Yes! Live chat is available during business hours by clicking the chat icon in the sidebar. For urgent matters, you can reach our phone support line at (555) 123-4567. Standard tickets through this portal are best for non-urgent issues that require detailed documentation.
How can I provide feedback on my support experience?
+
After your ticket is resolved, you'll receive an email with a satisfaction survey. Your feedback helps us improve our service! You can also rate individual support interactions directly in the ticket interface.
