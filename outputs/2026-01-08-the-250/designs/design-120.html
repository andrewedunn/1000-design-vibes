*** FAQ ***

Can I switch plans anytime?
Yeah, no lock-in here. Upgrade or downgrade whenever. We're not your parents. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards, debit cards, and we support international currencies. We process through Stripe, so your payment info is secure (even if our aesthetic isn't corporate).
Is there a setup fee?
Nope. Zero. Zilch. We don't believe in hidden fees. What you see is what you pay. That's the whole anti-establishment thing.
Can I use my own domain?
Pro and Empire plans let you use custom domains. Starter plans get a subdomain (yourname.phantomcore.io). It's not ideal but it's honest work for the price.
What if I exceed my order limit?
We'll email you when you hit 80%. After that, $0.10 per extra order on Starter, $0.08 on Pro. Or just upgrade. Your call. We're flexible like that.
Do you offer refunds?
30-day money-back guarantee if you're not feeling it. After that, no refunds but you can cancel anytime. We're rebels with ethics.
