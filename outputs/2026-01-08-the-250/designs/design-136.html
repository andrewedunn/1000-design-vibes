EducationForward Launches Revolutionary Open Access Learning Platform

New initiative provides free, accredited courses to 10 million students globally, breaking down barriers to quality education

EducationForward today announced the launch of OpenLearn Global, a groundbreaking open-access learning platform designed to democratize education for learners worldwide. The platform launches with over 500 accredited courses across 30 disciplines, all available free of charge to students in underserved communities.

The initiative represents a $100 million commitment over five years to transform educational access. OpenLearn Global partners with 75 leading universities and institutions to deliver curriculum that meets rigorous academic standards while remaining accessible to students regardless of geographic or economic circumstances.

"Education is not a privilege—it's a fundamental right," said Dr. Sarah Chen, Founder and CEO of EducationForward. "OpenLearn Global dismantles the traditional barriers that have kept quality education out of reach for millions of talented, motivated learners around the world."

"This platform represents the future of learning—accessible, flexible, and uncompromising in quality. We're not just offering courses; we're opening doors."
— Dr. Sarah Chen, Founder & CEO, EducationForward

The platform features innovative learning tools including adaptive assessment technology, peer collaboration spaces, and mentorship matching algorithms that connect students with professionals in their fields of interest. All courses offer official certificates of completion that are recognized by major employers worldwide.

OpenLearn Global is optimized for low-bandwidth environments and includes offline-capable mobile applications, ensuring students in remote areas with limited internet connectivity can still access world-class educational content.

Initial beta testing across six countries demonstrated remarkable results, with completion rates 40% higher than traditional online learning platforms and student satisfaction scores averaging 4.7 out of 5 stars.

About EducationForward

EducationForward is a non-profit organization dedicated to expanding access to quality education worldwide. Founded in 2018, the organization has partnered with educational institutions, technology companies, and philanthropic organizations to create innovative solutions that break down barriers to learning. EducationForward's programs have reached over 5 million students across 120 countries, providing resources, mentorship, and pathways to accredited education. The organization is headquartered in San Francisco with regional offices in London, Singapore, and Nairobi.
