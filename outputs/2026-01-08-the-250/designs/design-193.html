RECOMMENDATIONS

  • Monitor symptoms over the next 24-48 hours
  • Stay hydrated and get adequate rest
  • Use over-the-counter pain relief as needed
  • Schedule routine check-up with primary care physician if symptoms persist
  • Keep a symptom diary noting changes in intensity or new symptoms

WHEN TO SEEK IMMEDIATE CARE

  • Symptoms suddenly worsen or become severe
  • Difficulty breathing or chest pain develops
  • High fever (>103°F/39.4°C) that doesn't respond to medication
  • Signs of dehydration (extreme thirst, dark urine, dizziness)
  • New confusion or inability to stay awake
