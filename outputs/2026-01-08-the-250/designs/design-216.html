Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can make a tax-deductible donation through our secure online portal, by mailing a check, or by setting up recurring monthly gifts. All donations are processed through our certified payment system, and you'll receive an immediate email receipt for tax purposes. Our organization is registered as a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, and donations are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law.
We offer diverse volunteer opportunities including direct program support, administrative assistance, event coordination, and specialized skills-based volunteering. Visit our Volunteer Programs section to browse current openings, read position descriptions, and submit an application. Most positions require a brief orientation and background check.
We maintain full transparency through our Impact & Reporting section, where you can access annual reports, audited financial statements, program outcome metrics, and quarterly updates. All documents are available as downloadable PDFs. We also publish our IRS Form 990 annually and maintain ratings with charity watchdog organizations.
Yes, we gratefully accept in-kind donations of goods and services that align with our mission. Common needs include office supplies, technology equipment, professional services, and program-specific materials. Please contact our donations team before sending items to ensure they match current needs and can be properly received and acknowledged.
Partnership and grant applications are accepted on a rolling basis through our online application portal. Review the eligibility requirements in the Partnerships section, prepare the required documentation, and submit your proposal through the designated form. Our team reviews applications quarterly and will contact you within 4-6 weeks of submission.
Our Events & Programs calendar is updated weekly with fundraising events, community programs, educational workshops, and volunteer opportunities. You can filter by event type, date, or location. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to receive event notifications and early registration access.
