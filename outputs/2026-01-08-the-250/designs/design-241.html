Cookie Consent Design System

A psychedelic-inspired, friendly approach to cookie consent for educational platforms. This design transforms the often-stressful privacy decision into an approachable, visually engaging experience.

Groovy Aesthetics

60s/70s psychedelic influences meet modern accessibility standards. Organic blob shapes, warm earth tones, and flowing typography create a friendly, non-threatening consent experience.

  • Wavy dividers and curved borders
  • Offset groovy shadows
  • Gradient accents
  • Playful asymmetry

Educational Trust

Designed specifically for learning platforms where transparency and user empowerment matter. Clear explanations, equal visual weight for accept/reject, and granular control.

  • Plain language descriptions
  • No dark patterns
  • Category-level controls
  • Privacy-first defaults

Design Principles

International Minimalism: Despite the psychedelic inspiration, the layout remains clean and uncluttered. Generous whitespace and clear hierarchy ensure the design works across cultures and languages.

Natural Undertones: The earth-toned palette (terracotta, sand, amber) grounds the vibrant accents, creating warmth without overwhelming users. This natural base makes privacy decisions feel organic, not transactional.

Accessibility First: All interactive elements meet 48px minimum touch targets. Focus states are bold and visible. Color is never the only indicator. Reduced motion preferences are respected.

No Dark Patterns: Accept and reject buttons have equal visual weight. Required cookies are clearly labeled. All categories can be toggled individually. Privacy policy is always accessible.
