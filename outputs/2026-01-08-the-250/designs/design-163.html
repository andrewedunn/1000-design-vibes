Spritz Cosmo Launches New Sustainable Seltzer Line, Redefining Organic Refreshment

Natural spirits brand introduces botanical-infused sparkling beverages in 100% recycled packaging, bringing artisanal quality to everyday hydration with zero artificial ingredients

Spritz Cosmo, the Pacific Northwest's leading natural beverage company, today announced the launch of its groundbreaking Botanical Essence Collection‚Äîa line of premium sparkling seltzers crafted with organic botanicals and packaged in industry-leading sustainable materials. The collection marks a significant milestone in the company's mission to bring authentic, artisanal refreshment to conscious consumers nationwide.

The new collection features six distinctive flavors: Lavender Citrus, Rosemary Grapefruit, Cucumber Mint, Elderflower Lime, Sage Lemon, and Chamomile Orange. Each flavor is meticulously crafted using only certified organic ingredients, with no artificial sweeteners, preservatives, or added sugars. The beverages are packaged in aluminum cans made from 100% recycled content and printed with plant-based inks.

"We founded Spritz Cosmo on the belief that everyday refreshment shouldn't require compromise," said Maya Chen, Co-Founder and Chief Product Officer. "Our customers shouldn't have to choose between taste, health, and environmental responsibility. With the Botanical Essence Collection, they can enjoy all three in perfect harmony."

The launch comes at a pivotal moment for the beverage industry, as consumers increasingly seek transparency and sustainability from their favorite brands. According to recent market research, 73% of millennials and Gen Z consumers are willing to pay premium prices for products that align with their environmental values. Spritz Cosmo has positioned itself at the forefront of this movement, with the new collection representing a 40% reduction in carbon footprint compared to traditional beverage production methods.

Each Botanical Essence flavor was developed over an 18-month period in collaboration with master herbalists and flavor scientists. The company sources its botanicals from certified organic farms across the Pacific Northwest and uses a proprietary cold-infusion process that preserves the delicate aromatic compounds and essential oils of each ingredient. The result is a remarkably authentic taste profile that captures the true essence of fresh herbs and fruits.

The Botanical Essence Collection will be available starting February 1, 2026, through the company's direct-to-consumer website and at select natural grocery retailers across the United States. A variety 12-pack featuring all six flavors will retail for $24.99, with individual 8-packs available for $16.99. The company is also launching a subscription service offering 15% savings for regular deliveries.

About Spritz Cosmo

Founded in 2021 in Portland, Oregon, Spritz Cosmo is a natural beverage company committed to creating delicious, sustainable refreshment without compromise.
