Privacy Statement and Certification

By submitting this enrollment application, you certify that all information provided is true and accurate to the best of your knowledge. You understand that false statements may result in denial of admission or dismissal from the educational program.

The Educational Records Bureau collects this information in accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and applicable state education laws. Your personal information will be maintained securely and used only for enrollment processing, academic records, and communication regarding your child's education.

You have the right to review educational records, request corrections, and control disclosure of personally identifiable information as provided under FERPA. For questions about data privacy practices, contact the Records Office at privacy@educationalbureau.gov or (555) 012-3456.
