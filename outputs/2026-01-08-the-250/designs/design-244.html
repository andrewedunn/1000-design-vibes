Healthcare Support Chat Widget

This live chat widget embodies Art Deco elegance with a healthcare focus. The design uses muted pewter tones and natural earth colors to create a calming, trustworthy environment for patient support conversations. Geometric patterns and stepped borders pay homage to 1920s glamour while maintaining professional restraint.

Click the chat bubble in the bottom right to interact with the widget. Notice the Art Deco geometric patterns on the chat bubble, the stepped border decorations, and the elegant serif typography that conveys timeless quality. The design supports both light and dark modes, maintaining its nostalgic character across contexts.

  • Chat bubble with Art Deco geometric pattern
  • Agent info header with professional healthcare context
  • Message history with decorative corner accents
  • Typing indicator with animated dots
  • Quick response buttons with Art Deco frames
  • Input area with attachment support and stepped borders
  • Smooth animations and transitions
  • Full keyboard navigation support
