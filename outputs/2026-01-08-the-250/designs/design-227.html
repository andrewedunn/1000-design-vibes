Merchant Terms of Service Agreement

Between Core Marketplace Platform, Inc. ("Platform")
and Merchant Entity ("Merchant")

Preamble

WHEREAS, Core Marketplace Platform, Inc. ("Platform") operates an online e-commerce marketplace connecting merchants with consumers globally, providing technology infrastructure, payment processing, and customer acquisition services;

WHEREAS, Merchant desires to utilize Platform's services to offer products for sale to consumers through the Platform's marketplace infrastructure;

WHEREAS, the parties wish to establish the terms and conditions governing Merchant's use of Platform services, including obligations, fees, intellectual property rights, and dispute resolution procedures;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

I.

Definitions

1.1 "Platform"
shall mean Core Marketplace Platform, Inc., its subsidiaries, affiliates, and assigns, including all technology infrastructure, services, and marketplace operations.
1.2 "Merchant"
shall mean any individual, partnership, corporation, or legal entity that registers to sell products or services through the Platform.
1.3 "Products"
shall mean all goods, services, digital content, or other items offered for sale by Merchant through the Platform.
1.4 "Transaction"
shall mean any completed purchase of Products by a consumer through the Platform, including payment processing and order fulfillment.
1.5 "Merchant Dashboard"
shall mean the web-based interface provided to Merchant for managing product listings, orders, inventory, and account settings.
II.

Merchant Obligations

2.1

Product Listing Accuracy

Merchant shall ensure that all product listings, descriptions, images, and specifications are accurate, complete, and not misleading. Merchant warrants that all Products comply with applicable laws, regulations, and safety standards.

2.2

Order Fulfillment

2.2.1 Merchant shall fulfill orders within the timeframe specified in product listings, not to exceed:
  • (a) Five (5) business days for domestic orders;
  • (b) Fifteen (15) business days for international orders;
  • (c) Timeframes explicitly stated for custom or made-to-order Products.
2.2.2 Merchant shall maintain accurate inventory levels in the Merchant Dashboard.
2.3

Customer Service

Merchant shall provide responsive customer service, responding to customer inquiries within twenty-four (24) hours and resolving disputes in good faith. Merchant shall honor Platform's customer satisfaction policies, including return and refund procedures.

2.4

Prohibited Products

Merchant shall not list or sell: (a) illegal goods or services; (b) counterfeit or unauthorized replicas; (c) hazardous materials without proper documentation; (d) products infringing third-party intellectual property rights; or (e) any items prohibited by Platform's Acceptable Use Policy.

III.

Platform Services

3.1

Marketplace Infrastructure

Platform shall provide Merchant with access to its e-commerce marketplace, including product listing tools, search and discovery features, customer traffic, and branded marketplace presence.

3.2

Payment Processing

Platform shall process all customer payments, handle payment security compliance, manage chargebacks and disputes, and remit proceeds to Merchant according to the payment schedule defined in Article IV.

3.3

Merchant Dashboard

Platform shall provide Merchant with access to a comprehensive dashboard for managing listings, viewing analytics, processing orders, and accessing support resources. Platform reserves the right to modify dashboard features with reasonable notice.

IV.

Fees & Payment Terms

4.1

Commission Structure

Merchant shall pay Platform a commission of twelve percent (12%) of the gross transaction value for each completed Transaction. Commission is calculated before taxes and shipping fees charged to customers.

4.2

Payment Processing Fees

In addition to commission, Merchant shall pay payment processing fees of 2.9% plus $0.30 per Transaction to cover third-party payment processor costs.

4.3

Payment Schedule

Platform shall remit payments to Merchant on a bi-weekly basis, covering all Transactions completed during the previous fourteen (14) day period, minus applicable fees, refunds, and chargebacks. Payment shall be made via ACH transfer to Merchant's designated bank account.

4.4

Reserve Account

Platform may withhold up to fifteen percent (15%) of Transaction proceeds in a reserve account to cover potential refunds, chargebacks, and disputes. Reserve funds shall be released ninety (90) days after the corresponding Transaction date.

V.

Intellectual Property Rights

5.1

Merchant Content License

Merchant grants Platform a worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, distribute, and display all product images, descriptions, trademarks, and content provided by Merchant for the purpose of operating the marketplace and promoting Merchant's products.

5.2

Platform Intellectual Property

All Platform technology, software, algorithms, design elements, and trademarks remain the exclusive property of Platform. Merchant receives no ownership rights or licenses beyond access necessary to use Platform services.

5.3

Infringement Claims

Merchant warrants that Products do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights. Merchant shall defend, indemnify, and hold Platform harmless from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from intellectual property infringement by Merchant's Products.

VI.

Liability & Indemnification

6.1

Limitation of Liability

PLATFORM'S TOTAL LIABILITY TO MERCHANT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE AMOUNT OF FEES PAID BY MERCHANT TO PLATFORM IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM. PLATFORM SHALL NOT BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

6.2

Merchant Indemnification

Merchant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Platform from any claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from: (a) Merchant's Products; (b) Merchant's breach of this Agreement; (c) Merchant's violation of applicable laws; or (d) infringement of third-party rights by Merchant.

6.3

Product Liability

Merchant assumes all liability for Products, including product defects, safety issues, warranty claims, and compliance with consumer protection laws. Platform acts solely as a marketplace intermediary and assumes no liability for Products sold by Merchant.

VII.

Termination

7.1

Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement with thirty (30) days written notice. Upon termination, Merchant shall fulfill all outstanding orders and Platform shall remit all outstanding payments minus applicable fees.

7.2

Termination for Cause

Platform may immediately terminate this Agreement if Merchant: (a) materially breaches any provision; (b) engages in fraudulent activity; (c) repeatedly receives negative customer feedback; (d) violates applicable laws; or (e) damages Platform's reputation or operations.

7.3

Effect of Termination

Upon termination, Merchant's access to Platform services shall cease immediately. Provisions regarding fees, intellectual property, liability, and dispute resolution shall survive termination. Merchant shall remain liable for all obligations incurred prior to termination.

VIII.

General Provisions

8.1

Governing Law & Jurisdiction

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York, without regard to conflict of law principles. Any disputes shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in New York County, New York.

8.2

Amendment

Platform may amend this Agreement with thirty (30) days notice to Merchant. Continued use of Platform services after the effective date of amendments constitutes acceptance. Merchant may terminate the Agreement if amendments are materially adverse.

8.3

Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect. The invalid provision shall be modified to the minimum extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable.

8.4

Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, understandings, and agreements. No modification shall be effective unless in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

8.5

Force Majeure

Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform obligations due to circumstances beyond reasonable control, including acts of God, war, terrorism, pandemics, government actions, or infrastructure failures. Obligations shall be suspended during the force majeure event.
