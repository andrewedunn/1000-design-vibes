Alignment & Hierarchy

Justified Text Alignment

Phantom Blend uses justified alignment to maximize the mysterious, fashion-forward aesthetic. Each word has purpose. Each line has weight. The brutalist foundation demands precision, while Memphis playfulness softens the edges. Dashed borders and pill-shaped elements prevent sterility. Geometric patterns provide rhythm. Earth tones create warmth despite the dark palette. This is design that whispers rather than shouts—sophisticated, deliberate, unforgettable.

Whitespace as Hierarchy

Ultra-airy density means breathing room between every element. Generous margins and padding create visual hierarchy through absence as much as presence. The eye navigates through space. Typography scale (minor third ratio) adds rhythm. Accent colors punctuate meaning. Focus states ensure accessibility. This design system balances mystery with usability, raw authenticity with refined elegance.
