The Future of Design Systems

Time
9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
Duration
60 minutes
Location
Main Hall A
Track
Design

Join us for an in-depth exploration of how design systems are evolving in 2026. We'll cover the latest tools, techniques, and methodologies that are shaping the future of collaborative design work. This session will include live demonstrations, case studies from leading tech companies, and interactive discussions about best practices.

Key topics include: component architecture, design tokens, automated documentation, version control strategies, and cross-platform consistency. Whether you're just starting with design systems or looking to level up your existing practice, this session will provide valuable insights and actionable takeaways.

About the Speaker

SM

Sarah Mitchell

Design Lead @ Figma

Sarah is a design systems expert with over 10 years of experience building scalable design infrastructure. At Figma, she leads the design systems team and has been instrumental in developing tools that help thousands of designers collaborate more effectively. She's a frequent speaker at design conferences and contributes to open-source design tools.
