OPERATIVE DOSSIER

Agent Frost emerged from the crystalline mountains of the Northern Territories, where ancient ice holds secrets older than civilization itself. Recruited by the Global Crystal initiative at a young age, their natural affinity for mysterious energies marked them as exceptional. Through rigorous training in both traditional statecraft and arcane disciplines, Agent Frost has become one of the division's most capable operatives. Their missions often involve investigating phenomena that blur the line between the mundane and the magical, protecting the realm from threats both seen and unseen. With a calm demeanor that belies their formidable capabilities, Agent Frost serves as a bridge between the old world of mystery and the new age of institutional order.
