üåô Moonlit Baklava

Preparation Time: 2 hours | Serves: 12

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb phyllo dough
  • 2 cups walnuts, finely chopped
  • 1 cup pistachios, crushed
  • 1 cup butter, melted
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup honey
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Rose water (3 drops under full moon)

Instructions: Layer phyllo sheets with butter and nut mixture. Bake until golden. Drizzle with honey syrup infused with rose water collected during the full moon for maximum mystical potency.

About Modern Mystic

Welcome to Modern Mystic, your portal for authentic Middle Eastern recipes with a touch of magic! I started this site in 1998 to share the wonderful recipes I learned from my grandmother in Damascus.

Mission: To preserve ancient culinary traditions and share the mystical food wisdom of the Middle East with the world.

Philosophy: Every ingredient has a story, every recipe holds power, and every meal brings people together.

This site is updated regularly with new recipes, cooking tips, and stories from the mystical food traditions of the Middle East. Bookmark this page and check back often!
