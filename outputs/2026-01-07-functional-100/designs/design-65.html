Getting Started

▼

Begin your floor plan by selecting a drawing tool from the toolbar. Use the Rectangle tool to create room outlines, then add walls, doors, and windows. Click and drag on the canvas to place elements. All measurements are displayed automatically in feet and inches.

Layer Management

▼

Toggle layers on and off to focus on specific aspects of your design. Hide furniture to work on walls, or turn off measurements for a cleaner presentation view. Each layer can be locked to prevent accidental edits.

Measurements & Scale

▼

The current scale is 1/4" = 1'0", with a grid spacing of 1 foot. Use the Measure tool to add custom dimensions between any two points. The scale indicator at the bottom left shows the relationship between screen distance and real-world measurements.

Export Options

▼

Export your floor plan as PNG, PDF, or DWG format. PDF exports include all layers and maintain vector quality for printing. DWG files are compatible with AutoCAD and other professional CAD software.
