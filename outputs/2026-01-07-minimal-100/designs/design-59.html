Sample 7-Day Itinerary: Bali Discovery

Day 1

Arrival & Ubud Welcome

Touch down in Denpasar, transfer to Ubud. Explore the Monkey Forest Sanctuary and visit traditional art markets. Enjoy a welcome dinner with authentic Balinese cuisine.

Day 2

Rice Terraces & Temple Tour

Visit the iconic Tegalalang Rice Terraces at sunrise. Tour the sacred Tirta Empul water temple and participate in a traditional purification ritual. Afternoon at leisure to explore local cafes.

Day 3

Volcano Sunrise Trek

Early morning trek to Mount Batur summit to watch the sunrise over the volcanic landscape. Soak in natural hot springs afterward. Return for traditional Balinese massage.

Day 4

Beach Day in Seminyak

Transfer to coastal Seminyak. Relax on pristine beaches, try surfing lessons, or explore beachfront boutiques. Sunset cocktails at a beach club followed by fresh seafood dinner.

Day 5

Island Hopping Adventure

Day trip to Nusa Penida. Visit Kelingking Beach, snorkel at Crystal Bay, and explore hidden caves. Return to Seminyak for evening at leisure.

Day 6

Cultural Immersion

Visit Tanah Lot Temple at sunset. Attend a traditional Kecak dance performance. Participate in a Balinese cooking class and enjoy your homemade feast.

Day 7

Departure Day

Final morning for souvenir shopping or beach relaxation. Transfer to airport with memories to last a lifetime. Safe travels home!
