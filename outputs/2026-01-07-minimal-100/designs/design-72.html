About Me

I'm a developer who believes in the Bauhaus principle: form follows function. Every line of code serves a purpose, every interface element earns its place, and complexity is reduced to its essential elements.

With a decade of experience building web applications, I specialize in creating systems that are both powerful and intuitive. My work combines technical precision with design thinking, resulting in products that people actually enjoy using.

Currently focused on TypeScript, React, and Node.js, I'm passionate about accessibility, performance, and the intersection of art and technology.
