Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request maintenance for my property?
Submit a ticket above with the "Maintenance & Repairs" category. Include your property address, unit number, and detailed description. For emergencies, call our 24/7 hotline.
What's your average response time?
We respond to most tickets within 2-4 hours during business hours. Emergency maintenance requests are addressed immediately. You'll receive email updates as your ticket progresses.
Can I schedule a property viewing through the help desk?
Absolutely! Select "Property Viewings" as your category and let us know your preferred dates and times. Our leasing team will confirm availability within 24 hours.
How do I update my lease information?
Submit a ticket under "Lease & Contracts" and specify what changes you need. For lease renewals, we'll guide you through the process and provide all necessary documentation.
What if I have a payment dispute?
Choose "Payments & Billing" category and provide your account details and dispute reason. Our billing team will review and respond within 48 hours with a resolution.
