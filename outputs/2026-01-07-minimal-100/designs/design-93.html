Sound Design Concept Notes

JAN 03 2026 • 16:55
PRODUCTION

Silence as presence, not absence. When characters stop speaking, let environment speak: distant machinery, electromagnetic hum, the building settling. Observatory scenes need astronomical scale—vast emptiness with pinprick sounds. Radio static as character itself.
