01Parties and Recitals

This Creative Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2026 ("Effective Date"), by and between Clean Urban Studios LLC, a California limited liability company with its principal place of business at 1960 Pop Art Boulevard, Los Angeles, CA 90028 ("Studio"), and The Client, a creative entity seeking innovative design solutions ("Client").

Recitals

WHEREAS, Studio is engaged in the business of providing creative design services, brand development, and artistic direction with a distinctive aesthetic approach that merges natural elements with neutral foundations through a pop art lens;

WHEREAS, Client desires to retain Studio to provide certain creative services as more particularly described herein; and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to set forth the terms and conditions under which such services shall be performed.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

02Scope of Services

2.1 Creative Services

Studio shall provide Client with creative design services ("Services") that may include, but are not limited to:

  • Brand identity development and visual systems design
  • Graphic design for print and digital media
  • Art direction and creative consultation
  • Illustration and custom artwork creation
  • User interface and experience design
  • Motion graphics and animation services

2.2 Deliverables

All deliverables shall be specified in individual Statements of Work ("SOW") executed by both parties. Each SOW shall reference this Agreement and include detailed descriptions of deliverables, timelines, milestones, and compensation specific to that project.

Important Notice

All creative work shall embody the Studio's signature aesthetic philosophy: the harmonious integration of organic, natural elements with minimalist, neutral foundations, interpreted through a vibrant pop art perspective that emphasizes bold color, graphic clarity, and nostalgic cultural resonance.

03Compensation and Payment

3.1 Fees

Client shall compensate Studio for Services according to the fee structure outlined in each applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified, Studio's standard rates are as follows:

Service Category Hourly Rate Project Minimum
Art Direction $250/hour $5,000
Graphic Design $185/hour $3,000
Illustration $200/hour $4,000
UI/UX Design $225/hour $4,500

3.2 Payment Terms

All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. Studio reserves the right to charge interest on overdue amounts at a rate of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

3.3 Expenses

Client shall reimburse Studio for all reasonable and pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Services, including but not limited to stock photography, fonts, printing, and travel expenses.

04Intellectual Property Rights

4.1 Ownership of Deliverables

Upon Client's full payment of all fees and expenses due under this Agreement and the applicable SOW, Studio hereby assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in and to the final approved deliverables ("Final Deliverables") specifically created for Client under the applicable SOW.

4.2 Studio Retention Rights

Studio retains ownership of all preliminary designs, concepts, sketches, and materials not designated as Final Deliverables. Studio also retains the right to use any general knowledge, skills, experience, ideas, concepts, know-how, and techniques acquired or used in the course of performing Services.

4.3 Portfolio Rights

Studio reserves the right to reproduce, publish, and display the Final Deliverables in Studio's portfolio, websites, galleries, design publications, and other media solely for the purpose of Studio's self-promotion, unless Client objects in writing within thirty (30) days of project completion.

4.4 Third-Party Materials

Client acknowledges that certain elements incorporated into the Final Deliverables may be subject to third-party rights (including stock photography, fonts, and music). Studio shall identify such elements, and Client shall be responsible for securing appropriate licenses for their use.

05Revisions and Approvals

5.1 Revision Rounds

Each SOW shall specify the number of revision rounds included in the project fee. Studio shall make reasonable efforts to accommodate Client's revision requests within the scope of the original creative brief. Additional revisions beyond those specified in the SOW shall be billed at Studio's standard hourly rates.

5.2 Client Approval

Client shall review and approve or provide specific written feedback on deliverables within seven (7) business days of submission. Failure to respond within this timeframe shall constitute acceptance of the deliverable as submitted.

5.3 Scope Changes

Any changes to the scope of Services described in an SOW must be requested in writing and approved by both parties. Studio shall provide a written estimate of additional fees and time required for such changes before commencing work on the revised scope.

06Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

Each party agrees to maintain in confidence all proprietary and confidential information of the other party disclosed in connection with this Agreement ("Confidential Information"). Confidential Information shall not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully in the receiving party's possession prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully obtained from a third party without restriction.

The receiving party shall use the same degree of care to protect Confidential Information as it uses to protect its own confidential information of similar nature, but in no event less than reasonable care. This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

07Term and Termination

7.1 Term

This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until terminated by either party in accordance with this Section.

7.2 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW upon thirty (30) days' written notice to the other party. Upon such termination, Client shall pay Studio for all Services performed and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, plus a termination fee equal to 25% of the fees for Services not yet performed under active SOWs.

7.3 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within fifteen (15) days of receiving written notice thereof.

7.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination, Client shall pay all outstanding fees and expenses. Studio shall deliver all Final Deliverables completed as of the termination date, and the parties shall cooperate to ensure an orderly transition.

08Warranties and Representations

8.1 Studio Warranties

Studio represents and warrants that: (a) it has the full right and authority to enter into this Agreement and perform the Services; (b) the Final Deliverables shall be original works created specifically for Client or properly licensed; (c) the Final Deliverables shall not infringe upon any third-party intellectual property rights; and (d) Services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards.

8.2 Client Warranties

Client represents and warrants that: (a) it has the authority to enter into this Agreement; (b) all materials provided to Studio for incorporation into deliverables are either owned by Client or properly licensed; and (c) use of such materials as directed by Client shall not infringe any third-party rights.

8.3 Disclaimer

EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY PROVIDED HEREIN, STUDIO MAKES NO WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING WITHOUT LIMITATION ANY IMPLIED WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY OR FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE. STUDIO DOES NOT WARRANT THAT THE DELIVERABLES WILL ACHIEVE ANY PARTICULAR BUSINESS RESULT OR GENERATE ANY SPECIFIC LEVEL OF REVENUE OR PROFIT.

09Limitation of Liability and Indemnification

9.1 Limitation of Liability

IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT, EVEN IF SUCH PARTY HAS BEEN ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES. STUDIO'S TOTAL LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID BY CLIENT TO STUDIO IN THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE EVENT GIVING RISE TO LIABILITY.

9.2 Mutual Indemnification

Each party shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the other party from and against any claims, damages, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising from: (a) any breach of its representations, warranties, or obligations under this Agreement; or (b) its negligence or willful misconduct.

10General Provisions

10.1 Independent Contractor

Studio is an independent contractor, not an employee of Client. Studio shall have sole control over the manner and means of performing the Services, subject to the specifications in each SOW. Nothing herein shall create a partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship between the parties.

10.2 Assignment

Neither party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other party, except that either party may assign this Agreement to a successor in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets.

10.3 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of California, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Los Angeles County, California.

10.4 Entire Agreement

This Agreement, together with any SOWs executed hereunder, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings, whether written or oral, relating to such subject matter.

10.5 Amendment

This Agreement may be amended only by a written instrument signed by both parties.

10.6 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect, and such invalid or unenforceable provision shall be reformed to the extent necessary to make it valid and enforceable while preserving its intent.

10.7 Notices

All notices required or permitted under this Agreement shall be in writing and delivered by email, certified mail, or overnight courier to the addresses set forth in the first paragraph of this Agreement or as otherwise designated by either party in writing.
