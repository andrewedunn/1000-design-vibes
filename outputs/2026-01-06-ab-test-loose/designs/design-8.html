Design Principles

Where psychedelic meets Scandinavian restraint

Glassmorphism

Frosted glass surfaces with backdrop blur create ethereal layers. Double borders and inset shadows enhance the dimensional quality while maintaining transparency.

backdrop-filter: blur(20px)

"Surfaces float above each other, creating depth through blur and light rather than solid shadows."

Duotone Gradients

Coral-to-peach and peach-to-lavender create vibrant focal points that energize the interface.

Psychedelic Energy

60s-inspired flowing gradients and unexpected color combinations bring creative energy. Geometric patterns in the background echo the era's experimental spirit while maintaining modern usability.

Experimental Mind-bending

Scandinavian Restraint

Despite the vibrant colors, balanced density and generous white space create breathing room. Very rounded corners soften the interface, making it approachable rather than overwhelming.
