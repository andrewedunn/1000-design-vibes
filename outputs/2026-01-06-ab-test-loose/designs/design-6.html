Design Principles

  • Grid Precision: Four-column layout derived from Swiss International tradition ensures mathematical harmony
  • Color Hierarchy: Dramatic contrast between warm metallics and deep backgrounds guides attention
  • Generous Spacing: Loose line heights and letter spacing create breathing room, inspired by Japanese ma
  • Gradient Meshes: Subtle radial gradients add depth without breaking the flat paradigm
  • Typographic Elegance: Modern and old style serifs work together for sophistication and readability
