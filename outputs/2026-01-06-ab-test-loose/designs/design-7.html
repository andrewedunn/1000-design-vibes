Design Principles

Ultra-Dense Spacing

4px base unit creates tight, information-rich layouts. Every pixel is intentional. No wasted space.

Honest Materials

Noise texture, dashed borders, and subtle gradients evoke paper, fabric, and craft materials.

Asymmetric Balance

Freeform grid system allows organic, collage-like compositions that feel human-made.

Neon Warmth

Hyper-saturated neons provide emotional warmth in an otherwise neutral palette—nostalgic signage meets modern UI.

Low Contrast Comfort

Low contrast creates a softer, more nostalgic feel—easier on the eyes, welcoming to seniors.

Japanese Simplicity

Wabi-sabi appreciation for imperfection, asymmetry, and humble materials guides every decision.
