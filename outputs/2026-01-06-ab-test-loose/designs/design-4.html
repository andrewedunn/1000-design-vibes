ECHO CORE

Bauhaus geometry meets neon warmth. An editorial design system balancing ultra-dense information architecture with very-rounded, approachable surfaces.

Design System Architecture

BAUHAUS ERA EDITORIAL PARADIGM WARM NEON

Echo Core combines strict Bauhaus geometry with editorial typography sensibilities. The system prioritizes hierarchy through size, not color, while maintaining extreme contrast for accessibility. Ultra-dense information is softened by very-rounded corners, creating tension between form and function.

The palette uses desaturated neutrals with a warm neon orange accent, enabling vibrant emphasis without overwhelming the composition. Every element follows a minor third (1.067x) type scale, maintaining proportion at all sizes.

Color System

Neutral foundation with warm neon accent. Extreme contrast ensures legibility in dense layouts.

Typography

Slab serif headings command attention. Neo-grotesque body text ensures clarity and rhythm.

Spacing

10px base unit creates tight, dense layouts. Very-rounded corners soften rigid geometry.
