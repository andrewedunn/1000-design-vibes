Asymmetric Layout

Broken Grid System

The layout breaks traditional symmetry with a mixed-width column grid (1fr, 1.4fr, 0.9fr). This creates visual interest while maintaining corporate structure and right-aligned emphasis. Spacing uses 12px base unit in compact density.

Elements flow with purpose: important content gets more breathing room, secondary content compressed to reinforce hierarchy through weight and position.

Design Personality

The Tension

Thunder Core merges contradictions: rigid corporate structure with soft, inviting forms. Energetic yet sophisticated. Playful yet trustworthy. American optimism tempered with Scandinavian restraint.

The name itself—Thunder + Core—evokes power and solidity, delivered through warm clay-like forms that feel approachable rather than intimidating.

This is a design for premium consumer SaaS: professional enough for business, playful enough to delight.
