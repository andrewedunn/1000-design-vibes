Design Principles üéØ

Honesty

No pretense. Raw HTML aesthetic. Every pixel serves a purpose. Sharp corners, double borders, and straightforward layouts reveal the structure beneath.

Accessibility First

Designed for seniors: high contrast text, clear focus states, readable typography at 16px minimum, 44px touch targets on all interactive elements.

Complementary Contrast

Orange and blue create visual interest despite low contrast. Hyper-saturated but restrained‚Äîintensity through subtlety, not overwhelming the page.

Single Column, Left Aligned

Predictable, scannable layout. No multi-column complexity. Left alignment respects reading patterns. Compact density means less scrolling.

Handwritten Warmth

Caveat typography on headings brings humanity to the stark geometric grid. A conversation between structure (Space Mono) and personality (Caveat).
