Design Principles

Skeuomorphic

Real-world material textures inform the palette. Metallic accents suggest luxury and precision.

Memphis Energy

Playful geometry with bold shapes. Duotone icons. Three-column ultra-dense layout celebrates information abundance.

Metallic Mood

Cool palette with silver accents. Monochromatic color scheme with extreme contrast. Every surface feels intentional.

Ultra-Dense

Information-rich layout with 8px base unit. Layered shadows create depth. Nothing wasted.

Three-Column

Grid-based layout with consistent 1.5x gap. Justified alignment. Responsive behavior.

Glassmorphic

Soft gradients and blurred effects. Duotone filter on icons. Layered shadow system.
