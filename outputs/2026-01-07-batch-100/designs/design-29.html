Global Blend

A flat approach meets american corporate sensibility

This design system combines the stark honesty of neo-brutalism with the refined restraint of american corporate aesthetics. Bold tetradic colors, ultra-airy spacing, and thick borders create a distinctive visual language rooted in clarity and confidence.

Core Principles

Flat Structure

No shadows or layered effects. Pure color, clean geometry, honest borders define the visual hierarchy.

Ultra-Airy Density

Generous spacing throughout. White space breathes. Elements stand alone with confident separation.

Tetradic Balance

Four jewel tones in perfect harmony: emerald, ruby, sapphire, and amber create visual richness without discord.

Neo-Brutalist Edge

Raw and uncompromising. Thick borders. Minimal decoration. Maximum clarity. Honest typography speaks volumes.
