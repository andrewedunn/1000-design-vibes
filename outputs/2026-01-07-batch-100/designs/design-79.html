Veil Duo

Primary Meets Mixed: A Swiss International Perspective

Design Philosophy

Veil Duo harnesses the precision of Swiss grid systems with glassmorphic transparency. Primary color energy (magenta, cyan, yellow) creates intensity against a dark canvas. Broken layouts defy rigid structure while maintaining clarity.
