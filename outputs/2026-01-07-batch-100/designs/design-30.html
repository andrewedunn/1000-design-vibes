Global Copper

Swiss International-inspired neumorphic system for SMB fitness industry. Combining geometric precision with warm, elegant sensibilities.

Neumorphic Depth

Dramatic shadows and soft extruded forms create layered visual hierarchy. Every element has tactile, physical presence while maintaining Swiss clarity.

Right-Aligned Hierarchy

Content flows right to left, emphasizing primary actions and information. Creates visual tension and directs focus with precision.

Warm Neutrality

Neutral palette with copper accents provides serious yet approachable tone. French elegance meets Swiss functionality.

Key Features

Very Rounded Corners

12px border radius softens all elements while maintaining geometric precision. Approachable yet controlled.

Dramatic Shadows

Layered, colored shadows (copper-tinted) create elevation and depth. Neumorphic surfaces feel touchable and present.

Loose Letter Spacing & Line Height

0.04em+ letter spacing and 1.8+ line height provide breathing room. Elegant and highly legible across all sizes.

Single-Column Layout

Content flows vertically with right-aligned emphasis. Directs focus, respects Swiss grid principles, creates visual rhythm.
