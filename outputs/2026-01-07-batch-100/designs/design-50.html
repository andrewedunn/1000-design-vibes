Raw Structure

Honest Borders

3px solid borders celebrate construction. No shadows, no depth tricks. What you see is what you get. Brutalism demands honesty—borders declare themselves proudly.

Pill Corners

50px radius softens brutal angles. Deco glamour meets brutalist honesty. The contradiction creates tension—sophisticated, not sterile.

Ultra-Airy Spacing

4px base unit creates dense measurements. But layout breathes through expansive 64px gaps. Maximum whitespace despite minimum measurements. A paradox that works.

Deco Symmetry

Low Contrast for Sophistication

Muted jewel tones on light background create subtle contrast. This isn't aggressive—it's refined. Premium brands understand: lower contrast signals luxury through restraint. 4:1 contrast might be technically accessible. Brutalist deco prefers whisper over shout.

Typography System

Archivo Black Headings

Geometric sans-serif in 900 weight creates authoritative presence. Uppercase and tight letter-spacing command attention. Deco geometric tradition meets modern brutalism.

IBM Plex Serif Body

Elegant serif maintains readability through generous line height (1.8). Classical typography supports luxury positioning. Modern serif feels contemporary without fashion.

Typographic Honesty

Both fonts declare their nature clearly. No subtlety about serif vs sans—they're different and announce that difference. Brutalism celebrates contradiction: soft pill curves with hard borders, muted colors with bold letterforms.

Spacing Philosophy

4px Base Unit

Tiny measurements might suggest claustrophobia. But brutalism celebrates architectural precision. 4px base allows for exact, intentional spacing at every scale.

64px Section Gap

Maximum breathing room between sections. Content floats with abundance of whitespace. The contradiction: tight base, airy layout. This is brutalist math.

Ultra-Airy Density

Despite 4px base, the overall experience feels open and luxurious. Negative space becomes the design's protagonist. Deco symmetry filled with silence.

Design Principles

Brutalist Honesty

Every element shows its structure. Borders don't hide—they declare. Colors don't seduce—they state. This is philosophy through design: say what you mean, mean what you say.

Deco Glamour

Art deco symmetry, jewel tones, geometric precision bring humanity to brutalism. Luxury lives in the contradiction between raw structure and refined aesthetics.

Maximum Restraint

Low contrast, muted colors, generous spacing. Brutalist deco proves sophistication demands less, not more. Premium brands speak quietly—let competitors shout.
