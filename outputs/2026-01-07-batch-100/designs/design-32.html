Design Principles

Editorial Hierarchy

Color and position establish reading order. Metallic accents guide attention through the composition. Typography-first approach shapes information architecture.

Broken Grid Aesthetics

Intentional asymmetry and offset columns create tension. Right-aligned text and mixed positioning reflect psychedelic energy without chaos.

Thick Borders, Hard Edges

3px borders with hard shadows create bold separation. Every element announces itself. Monochromatic palette with metallic accent provides contrast.

Dense Information Hierarchy

Compact 8px base unit. Small caps, very loose letter spacing. Every pixel serves purpose. Rhythm through repetition of borders and shadows.
