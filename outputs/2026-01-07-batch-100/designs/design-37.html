Design Principles

Claymorphic Forms

Soft, puffy, organic shapes. 20px border radius creates gentle, playful aesthetic. Dramatic shadows give depth and tactile presence.

Punk Energy

Bold borders, thick strokes, anti-establishment spirit. Triadic color theory with muted saturation. Chaotic joy beneath structured grid.

Pastel Mood

Soft, candy-like palette. Cool temperature. Low-to-medium contrast. Energetic despite gentle colors. Small caps typography reinforces playfulness.

Two-Column Balance

4px base unit with 8px gaps. Justified alignment creates rhythm. Paper texture adds handmade warmth. Balanced density through generous padding.

Component Showcase

Card Component

Claymorphic cards with colored borders. Hover effect lifts the element with enhanced shadow. Padding provides breathing room for content.

Flexible Grid

Responsive two-column layout. Stacks on mobile. Consistent spacing throughout. Each card maintains its unique personality while fitting the system.
