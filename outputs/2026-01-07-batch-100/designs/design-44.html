Freeform Components

Very Rounded

48px radius corners soften Bauhaus geometry. Very rounded shapes feel playful and approachable—perfect for gaming audiences.

Dashed Borders

Dashed lines add informality and energy. Breaks the rigid geometric aesthetic while maintaining clear visual boundaries.

Gradient Fills

Subtle gradients add depth without shadows. The flat design remains pure while gaining visual interest and dimensionality.

Playful Hover

On hover, cards rotate and scale. Movement feels natural and inviting—encouraging interaction and exploration.

Typography

Roboto Slab Headings

Bold, geometric serif creates strong visual hierarchy. Uppercase treatment amplifies impact. Perfect for game UI and action buttons.

Inter Body Text

Modern humanist sans-serif maintains readability amid colorful backgrounds. Supports tight line height (normal) for compact gameplay descriptions.

Tight Spacing, Airy Layout

4px base unit creates ultra-compact typography baseline. But elements float in abundant whitespace—open containers prevent claustrophobia. Playful contradiction: tight + airy.

Design Principles

Bauhaus Meets Play

Primary colors and geometric grids honor modernist traditions. But pastel softness and very rounded corners remind players this is fun, not serious.

Functional Beauty

Every shape serves a purpose. Grid systems guide layout. But color gradients and rounded borders ensure the interface feels alive and energetic.

Extreme Contrast for Clarity

Pastel backgrounds with dark text ensure 7:1+ contrast. Developers and children alike can read clearly—even in bright daylight gaming.
