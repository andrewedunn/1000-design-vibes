design principles

neumorphic

soft extruded forms and layered shadows create tactile depth without harsh contrasts. inset containers suggest physical depression.

art nouveau

organic blob shapes, flowing curves, and nature-inspired ornaments soften geometric structures and technical typography.

pastel palette

muted saturation reduces eye strain while maintaining visual interest. split complementary mauve and teal create sophisticated harmony.

airy spacing

generous gaps and loose letter spacing breathe room into layouts. very loose line height prevents crowding while maintaining readability.
