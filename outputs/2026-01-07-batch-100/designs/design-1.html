Antique Tundra

A editorial approach meets african bold sensibility

A distinctive dark editorial design system combining punk-era energy with nostalgic African bold aesthetics. Metallic cool tones, typography-driven hierarchy, and ultra-dense composition create a striking visual identity for senior audiences in educational contexts.
