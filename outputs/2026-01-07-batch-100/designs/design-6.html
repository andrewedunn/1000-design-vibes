design principles

skeuomorphic depth

real-world materials inform digital surfaces. fabric texture and dramatic shadows create tangible interfaces.

memphis geometry

playful shapes, extreme corners, and bold color combinations celebrate digital maximalism.

candy triadic

three equally-spaced hues (pink, cyan, yellow) on dark create vibrant, balanced contrast.

lowercase humanity

all text lowercase creates approachable, friendly tone. very loose letter spacing adds breathing room.

compact density

4px spacing base creates tight, intentional layouts. nothing feels wasted or empty.

cozy dark mode

dark background reduces eye strain while candy colors pop dramatically. hygge through contrast.
