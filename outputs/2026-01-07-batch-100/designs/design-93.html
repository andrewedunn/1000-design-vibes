Design Principles

Flat Design: No depth tricks, just bold shapes and honest typography. Punk Attitude: Raw, unpolished, refusing to follow rules. Cyber Aesthetics: Cool tones and desaturated color with high-contrast text. Ultra-Dense: Information-packed layouts that demand attention. Handwritten Warmth: Curvy fonts bring humanity to harsh edges. Paper Texture: Subtle grain suggests physical authenticity in a digital world.
