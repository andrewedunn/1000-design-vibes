design system summary

ui paradigm

brutalist: raw, stark, honest structure. bold borders, sharp corners, minimal ornamentation.

design era

pop art: bold, graphic, commercial irony. applies bright pastel tones and high contrast to catch attention.

key values

  • • density: ultra airy
  • • type scale: major third (1.25)
  • • corners: sharp (0px radius)
  • • borders: none (structure via contrast)

alignment strategy

mixed alignment: left, center, and right text placement creates dynamic visual rhythm while maintaining readability. hierarchy relies on size and color rather than position.
