Design Philosophy

Neumorphic Surfaces

Soft embossed effect with subtle light and shadow. Elements appear to float above or sink into the surface. 10px base unit creates gentle rhythm and breathing room.

Grunge Texture

Paper texture background adds handmade authenticity. Imperfections celebrate the organic nature of design. Transitional serif typography grounds the candy palette.

Candy Colors

Hyper-saturated pastel palette with complementary theory. Soft purple, pink, and blue create playful harmony. Neutral background lets colors shine.

Floating Containers

Modular grid with independent card components. Each element has breathing space. Consistent shadow treatment creates visual cohesion.
