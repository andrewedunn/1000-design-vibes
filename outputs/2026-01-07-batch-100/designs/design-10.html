What is Clash Core?

This is a punk-inspired healthcare design system that refuses corporate sterility. Built for small to medium healthcare providers who want trust with attitude. Every element carries texture, intention, and a slight edge. Colors clash beautifully. Typography commands respect. The design is ultra-dense, maximizing information while maintaining punk rebellion and earth authenticity.

Perfect for clinics, health tech startups, wellness centers, and any healthcare provider who believes good medicine deserves good design‚Äîraw, honest, and unapologetically bold.

Design Philosophy

üé® Tetradic Earth Tones

Four powerful colors clash with purpose: saddle brown anchors, dark olive provides forest depth, dark goldenrod brings sun energy, and Indian red adds warm rebellion. Together they create visual tension that keeps viewers engaged.

Roboto Slab headings command attention with edgy, strong presence. Montserrat body text ensures modern readability. Small-caps transformation makes every word bold and deliberately uniform. Golden ratio scaling (1.618x) creates natural visual hierarchy.

üì¶ Skeuomorphic Depth

Textured surfaces, double borders, colored shadows, and inset details create the illusion of physical objects. This realism builds trust in healthcare contexts while colored shadows add punk personality.

Surface & Texture

Borders

2px double borders create aggressive framing. This doubled style references punk DIY aesthetics and emphasizes separation between elements.

Shadows

Colored shadows (brown, olive, gold) rather than black. These are offset at 2‚Äì4px, creating skeuomorphic depth while maintaining punk rawness.

Texture

Noise overlays and diagonal stripe patterns add worn, authentic feel. These aren't perfectly smooth‚Äîthey're lived-in, trusted, real.

Corners

Subtle 4px radius (just a hint). Not friendly, not harsh. Slightly softened sharp edges mirror punk's refusal to be extreme.

Healthcare Use Cases

Patient Dashboard

Use earth tones for trusted baseline content, accent colors to highlight urgent items (prescriptions due, appointment reminders). Double borders frame each patient section. Texture creates visual depth without corporate polish.

Appointment Booking

Slab serif headers state appointment times clearly. Geometric sans body text keeps instructions readable. Golden ratio scaling ensures date/time fields are prominent. Colored shadows beneath buttons reward interaction.

Provider Directory

Ultra-dense single-column layout lists multiple providers. Emoji icons distinguish specialties. Justified alignment fills width. Clash Core refuses wasted space‚Äîevery pixel serves content.

Health Records Portal

Skeuomorphic containers resemble paper files. Double borders signal importance and security. Color hierarchy (brown for general, gold for labs, red for alerts) guides attention without corporate uniformity.
