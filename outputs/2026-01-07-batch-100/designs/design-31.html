Design Principles

Skeuomorphic

Real-world textures and materials inform every surface. Dashed borders and medium shadows create depth.

Art Deco

Geometric elegance meets organic curves. Symmetry balanced with freeform placement for movement.

Ultra Airy

Generous negative space throughout. 10px base unit allows breathing room between elements.

Mixed Alignment

Freeform grid system rejects rigid structure. Elements flow naturally like a jazz composition.

Typography

Heading (Lato, 700, 0.1em tracking)

Perfect fourth scale (1.333). Small caps optional. Loose letter spacing for elegance.

Body text in Lato. Very loose line height (1.8) and 0.05em letter spacing create a floating, spacious feel. This is the standard read experience across all components.
