Glassmorphic Surfaces

Frosted glass layers with backdrop blur create depth while maintaining readability. Hard shadows anchor elements in space.

Thick Borders

3px golden borders provide definition and luxury. High contrast ensures components pop against the dark background.

Compact Density

Tight spacing with intentional breathing room. Every element has purpose; nothing is wasted or overdone.

Hard Shadows

Layered offset shadows with golden undertones. Creates dimensional, almost engraved quality to surfaces.

Design Principles

Luxury Through Constraint

Limited, intentional color palette. Every decision serves the whole. Less is more.

Craft Heritage

Handwritten typography echoes Arts and Crafts movement. Human-made warmth in digital space.

Extreme Contrast

Dark grounds amplify jewel tones. High contrast ensures accessibility and visual impact.

Intentional Depth

Glassmorphism with hard shadows creates layered, dimensional interfaces without flatness.

Accessibility

Imperial Terra meets WCAG AA standards with:

  • 4.5:1+ contrast ratio across all text and interactive elements
  • Visible focus states with 3px golden outlines
  • 44px minimum touch targets for all buttons and interactive areas
  • Reduced motion support with @media prefers-reduced-motion
  • Semantic HTML for screen reader compatibility
