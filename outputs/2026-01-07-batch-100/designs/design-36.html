Design Principles

Skeuomorphic Texture

Real-world materials inform every surface. Double borders reference classic brutalism. Geometric patterns evoke handcrafted details and tactile warmth.

Neo-Brutalist Structure

Raw, honest design. Heavy borders, minimal ornamentation. Every element announces itself. Two-column layout creates balanced, intentional composition.

Mediterranean Spirit

Warm, earthy tones. Handwritten typography adds personal, human touch. Tight letter spacing reflects precision within organic sensibility.

Ultra-Airy Spacing

4px base unit allows breathing room despite visual weight. Generous gaps between elements. High contrast between filled and empty space.
