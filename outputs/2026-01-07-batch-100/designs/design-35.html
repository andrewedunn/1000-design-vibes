design principles

tetradic color theory

Four vibrant colors in perfect harmony. Hot pink, cyan, yellow, and electric blue create extreme contrast and maximum impact.

y2k nostalgia

Chrome effects, glossy surfaces, and geometric shapes recall early 2000s optimism. Colored shadows add dimension and playfulness.

material elevation

Layered surfaces with clear hierarchy. 10px base unit provides compact precision. Single-column layout maintains focus.

monospace typography

Slab serif headings and monospace body create technical, edgy aesthetic. Golden ratio scale (1.618) ensures readable hierarchy with generous whitespace.
