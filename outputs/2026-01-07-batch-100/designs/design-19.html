Design Principles

  • Scandinavian Minimalism: Ultra-airy spacing emphasizes clarity and breathing room
  • Claymorphic Depth: Soft shadows and rounded elements create tactile, approachable surfaces
  • Cool Palette: Desaturated blues and cool accents create serene, ice-like atmosphere
  • Complementary Tension: Primary blue paired with warm terracotta for subtle visual interest
  • Weight-Based Hierarchy: Left-aligned layout with font-weight changes guide focus
  • Fabric Texture: Subtle woven pattern adds tactile sophistication to surfaces
  • Accessible By Default: 4.5:1 minimum contrast, 44px touch targets, clear focus states
  • Responsive Grids: 3-column grid system that adapts to smaller viewports
