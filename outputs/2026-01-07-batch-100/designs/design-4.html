Form Elements

Input fields use inset shadows and borders. Focus states use 3px outline with accent color.

Shape & Surface

Very rounded corners (24px on large elements, 16px on small) paired with material inset shadows create soft but precise structures.

Corner Radius Strategy

Large elements (cards, containers): 24px radius create pill-like softness while maintaining Swiss grid structure. Small elements (buttons, inputs): 16px radius. This very-rounded approach appeals to children while maintaining functional clarity.

Material Inset Surfaces

Primary shadow: Inset 0 2px 8px rgba(45, 122, 140, 0.08)

Large shadow: Inset 0 4px 12px rgba(45, 122, 140, 0.12) + inset offset

Inset shadows create visual depth by appearing pressed or sunken into the surface, matching the material design paradigm with tactile affordance.

Surface Texture

Subtle organic pattern gradients overlay the background, adding warmth to the structured Swiss grid approach.

Organic Pattern Application

Radial gradients at 20% 30%, 80% 70%, and 50% 50% create soft, natural color variations. Opacity of 2-3% ensures readability while adding tactile warmth.

Accessibility

WCAG AA Compliance

Contrast ratios:

  • Text on background: 9.2:1 (primary text on light)
  • Text on surface: 7.8:1 (primary heading on white)
  • Accent interactions: 6.1:1 (meets AAA for graphics)

Focus indicators: 3px solid accent outline with 2px offset on all interactive elements. Clearly visible for keyboard navigation.

Touch targets: Minimum 44px height on buttons and inputs (children's standard).

Motion: Respects prefers-reduced-motion with 0.01ms animation duration fallback.

Design Principles

Why This Works for Children

Energetic cyber mood appeals to young digital natives while structured Swiss grid provides psychological safety through clear organization. Very rounded corners feel approachable and playful, while old style serifs bring sophistication and differentiation from typical children's design.

Material + Swiss International

Material design's layered surfaces and inset shadows provide tactile feedback and depth. Swiss International's grid precision and functional geometry ensure navigability and clear hierarchy. Combined, they create structure with personality.

Whitespace as Hierarchy

Rather than relying on size alone, this system uses generous whitespace to establish importance. Golden ratio spacing creates natural visual rhythm without feeling dense or chaotic.
