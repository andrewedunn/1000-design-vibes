Floating Components

Claymorphic surfaces with dramatic shadows:

Claymorphic Depth

Soft, layered shadows create puffy, clay-like appearance. Surfaces feel handmade and tactile without skeuomorphic detail.

Dramatic Elevation

Large shadow offset creates dramatic floating effect. Cards appear to levitate above the background. Perfect for luxury food.

Subtle Corners

8px radius maintains Scandinavian minimalism. Not rounded—just softened. Geometric precision with human warmth.

Monochromatic Accents

Top gradient bars guide visual hierarchy. Primary colors hint at identity without overwhelming premium aesthetic.

Freeform Layout

Asymmetric grid breaks rigid structure. Cards find their natural position—organic yet organized.

Dense Information

12px base spacing creates information-rich layouts. Justification and tight integration support luxury positioning.

Typography System

Bodoni Moda Headings

High-contrast serif creates luxury and sophistication. Uppercase treatment with letter spacing signals premium positioning. Perfect for food narrative.

Raleway Body Text

Elegant geometric sans-serif maintains readability. Supports justified layout for magazine-like presentation. Loose line height (1.6) keeps food content breathable.

Scandinavian Restraint

Both fonts respect minimalist principles. No artificial emphasis—hierarchy through size, color, and weight. The design speaks quietly, yet clearly. This is the essence of Scandinavian design: maximum impact through minimal intervention.

Design Principles

Luxury Through Simplicity

Scandinavian minimalism proves restraint costs more than decoration. Subtle details—8px radius, specific color harmony—signal craftsmanship and intention.

Claymorphic Warmth

Soft shadows and gradient surfaces create tactile presence. Food deserves to feel handmade. Claymorphism delivers that warmth without skeuomorphic excess.

Primary Confidence

Navy, red, gold feel authoritative together. Primary colors don't fight—they harmonize. Premium food brands can embrace primary color palettes without feeling childish.
