Typography System

Tangerine Script

Handwritten serif creates warm, personal presence. Perfect for headlines and accent text. Encourages trust through human connection.

Crimson Text

Elegant serif body font maintains readability while supporting the warm aesthetic. Normal line height (1.6) keeps layouts compact.

Mysterious Restraint

Lowercase default typography adds sophistication. Small caps and uppercase used sparingly for emphasis. The design whispers rather than shouts.

Design Principles

Organic Y2K Fusion

Blob shapes and chrome effects from Y2K meet earthy tones and handwritten warmth. The result: luxury that feels personal and mysterious.

Consumer Premium Trust

Muted colors and soft shadows create sophisticated, non-aggressive presence. Finance communicates security through subtlety, not flash.

Nostalgic Modernity

Y2K references appeal to millennials seeking finance. Organic shapes and handwriting differentiate from sterile fintech competitors.
