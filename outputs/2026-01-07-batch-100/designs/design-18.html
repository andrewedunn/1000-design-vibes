Design Principles

Airy Density

Extra spacing and breathing room between elements. Very loose line heights (2.1x) and letter spacing (1.2px) create comfortable reading experience.

Skeuomorphic Details

Subtle references to natural stone and Mediterranean craftsmanship. Rounded corners, soft shadows, and organic patterns evoke tactile quality.

Floating Container Style

Cards and surfaces appear to float above the background with subtle shadows. Creates depth without heavy layering or harsh contrast.

Natural Trustworthiness

Designed for children and families. Warm tones, clear hierarchy, and generous touch targets create safe, welcoming environment.

Spacing & Shapes

Spacing Scale

Base Unit: 8px

Scale: 8px → 16px → 24px → 32px → 40px → 48px → 64px

Creates consistent rhythm throughout. Generous margins and padding support the airy aesthetic.

Corner Radius

Default: 8px (subtle softness)

Rounded: 12px (prominent cards)

Pill: 999px (buttons, badges)

Accessibility Features

Contrast & Clarity

Extreme color contrast (monochromatic system) ensures text is readable for all users. 4.5:1 WCAG AA compliance throughout.

Interactive Focus States

Clear focus indicators on all interactive elements. Visible outlines help keyboard navigation and screen reader users.

Touch-Friendly

All touch targets are minimum 44x44 pixels. Generous spacing prevents accidental activation of wrong elements.

Reduced Motion Support

Respects user's motion preferences. Animations and transitions are disabled when OS requests reduced motion.
