Design Characteristics

  • ULTRA-DENSE LAYOUT

    Controlled spacing (4px base unit) with strategic whitespace. Left-aligned hierarchy with bold visual emphasis.

  • PILL-SHAPED CONTAINERS

    24px border radius creates soft, approachable forms. Combines geometric precision with organic curves.

  • THICK BORDERS & NO SHADOWS

    3px structural borders create clarity. No elevation shadows—structure comes from borders and color.

  • NOISE TEXTURE & MESH GRADIENTS

    Subtle noise pattern adds texture without distraction. Gradient meshes provide subtle dimensionality.

  • ACCESSIBLE FOR SENIORS

    Large typography (16px base), high contrast (extreme), readable monospace, 44px+ touch targets.

  • DARK MODE CYBERPUNK MOOD

    Deep charcoal with warm golden accents. Creates trustworthy yet modern aesthetic.
