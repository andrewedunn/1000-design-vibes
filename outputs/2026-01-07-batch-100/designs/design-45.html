Misty Core

Primary meets mixed: a mid century modern perspective

Component System

Dramatic Shadows

Hard, directional shadows create depth and tactile presence. Multi-layer shadows enhance 3D illusion.

Pill Corners

50px+ rounded corners reference pill shapes popular in mid-century design. Playful yet sophisticated.

Dense Layout

10px spacing base creates information-rich compositions. Small caps typography adds era-appropriate formality.

Skeuomorphic Depth

Textured surfaces and layered shadows make flat elements feel tangible. Children and fitness enthusiasts connect with this tactility.

Extreme Contrast

Primary colors on dark backgrounds create 8:1+ contrast. Energy and clarity work together in every interface.

Nostalgic Energy

Mid-century optimism meets modern usability. The past feels welcoming, not dusty or dated.

Typography System

Playfair Display Headings

Elegant serif typeface in small caps creates formal, era-appropriate presence. Uppercase treatment and letter spacing add grandeur.

Lato Body Text

Modern humanist sans-serif in small caps supports readability while honoring the era. Very loose line height (1.8) maximizes space on dense layouts.

Typographic Restraint

Despite dense spacing, typography never overwhelms. The system prioritizes hierarchy through color and weight, not size jumping.

Design Principles

Tactile Nostalgia

Skeuomorphic references—wood textures, soft shadows, dimensional surfaces—make digital interfaces feel real. Mid-century audiences loved this approach.

Primary Optimism

Red, blue, yellow represent post-war hope. The extreme contrast between primaries creates visual energy perfect for fitness motivation.

Dense Information

10px base spacing means more content per screen. But generous whitespace between sections prevents claustrophobia. Mid-century efficiency meets modern breathing room.
