Design Philosophy

Flat Design

No shadows except inset. Clean, direct visual language. Every element has clear purpose. 10px base unit provides rhythm and structure.

Arts & Crafts

Handcrafted sensibility meets digital precision. Double borders reference traditional design. Mixed temperature palette with extreme contrast.

Candy Colors

Hyper-vibrant palette with candy mood. Hot pink, gold, and deep pink create playful energy. Dark mode background lets colors shine.

Enterprise Serious

Despite playful palette, layout is serious and organized. Twelve-column grid. Right-aligned headings create hierarchy and movement.
