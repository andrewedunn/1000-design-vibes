Component Grid

Glass Surfaces

Frosted glass effect with backdrop blur creates layered depth. Semi-transparent surfaces feel modern and immaterial.

Neon Accents

Hyper-saturated colors punch through calm backgrounds. Hairline borders in complementary hues add visual texture.

Punk Asymmetry

Grid irregularity breaks expectations. Elements float and dance—nothing rigid, everything energetic.

Layered Shadows

Multiple colored shadows create depth. Each shadow uses primary or secondary colors for cohesion.

Organic Radius

18px rounded corners soften the digital sharpness. Punk softness paradoxically creates friendliness.

High Contrast

Dark text on light backgrounds with neon accents. Complementary color theory drives visual hierarchy.

Typography System

Space Mono for Impact

Monospace headings feel technical and punk. Uppercase treatment amplifies the attitude. Very loose letter spacing adds air.

Inter for Clarity

Humanist sans-serif body maintains readability amid visual chaos. Tight line height supports dense real estate content.

The Punk Philosophy

Typography breaks rules deliberately. Major second scale (1.125x) creates jarring jumps. This isn't comfortable—it's provocative.

Anti-design meets high function. Punk attitude, real estate ambition.

Design Principles

Visual Provocation

Neon colors and glass surfaces combine to create something unforgettable. Real estate marketing that refuses to blend in.

Structural Chaos

Asymmetric grids and staggered cards create motion and energy. Nothing feels mass-produced—everything feels custom and rebellious.

Transparent Honesty

Glassmorphism references honesty and visibility. Punk rejection of corporate obfuscation. Property details don't hide—they shine through glass.
