Typography

Headings: Crimson Text (Transitional Serif)

The Future Is Now
Bold, Uppercase Intent

Transitional serif headings in UPPERCASE command attention. Letter-spacing of 0.05em creates authentic grunge tension.

Body: Caveat (Handwritten)

This is where the authentic handwritten feel brings human chaos to the digital space. Grunge embraces imperfection and organic flow.

Handwritten body font (Caveat) adds DIY authenticity. Tight line-height (1.2) mirrors the density aesthetic.

Scale Ratio: Major Third

1rem → 1.5rem → 2.25rem → 3.375rem creates rhythmic hierarchy through proportional relationships.

Spacing & Density

Ultra-Dense Layout

Base unit: 10px. Tight spacing reflects grunge's layered, information-rich aesthetic.

Sharp Corners

0px border radius. Harsh geometry emphasizes raw honesty and structural clarity.

Inset Shadows

Depth created through inset shadows, suggesting carved or pressed surfaces.

Medium Borders

2px borders create visible structure and contain elements within the composition.

Accessibility

This design system maintains WCAG AA contrast standards (4.5:1 minimum). All interactive elements have visible focus states. Form inputs have minimum 44px height for touch targets. Motion-sensitive users benefit from reduced motion media queries.
