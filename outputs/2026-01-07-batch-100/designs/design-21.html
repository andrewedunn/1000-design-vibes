Design Features

Freeform Grid

Layout breaks free from rigid structure while maintaining ordered elegance. No predetermined template—design flows naturally.

Very Rounded Forms

32-40px border radius on all major containers creates friendly, organic shapes that soften the editorial formality.

Thick Borders

3px borders throughout provide bold visual definition. No shadows—pure form and line create structure.

Justified Text

Body text aligns on both edges, creating formal, editorial presentation. Pairs perfectly with slab serif headlines for print-like quality.
